# Results

## 3.1 Descriptive Statistics

A total of 300 synthetic interview transcripts were generated and scored, yielding 6458 question-response pairs for quality evaluation. The overall mean composite richness score was 3.06 (SD = 1.88) on a 0-5 scale. The mean latent dimension surfacing rate across all transcripts was 72.1%.

Across the five quality dimensions, mean scores were: emotional depth (M = 3.32), specificity (M = 2.95), latent surfacing (M = 3.28), narrative quality (M = 3.13), clinical grounding (M = 2.59).

## 3.2 Cross-Version Comparison
