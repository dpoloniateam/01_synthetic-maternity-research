## 8. Recommended Administration Protocol

1. Administer in semi-structured interview format (45-60 minutes)
2. Follow question order within each journey phase
3. Deploy all probes when initial responses are thin
4. Allow natural conversation flow — do not rigidly enforce question sequence
5. Adapt language for participant literacy and comfort level
